Privacy Policy for StoneSys

Last updated: July 2, 2026

StoneSys ("we", "our", or "the App") is a static web application designed as a companion character sheet helper for Stonetop/StoneSys RPG campaigns. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle user data and privacy.

1. No Server-Side Storage

StoneSys is a client-side static web application. We do not operate any database or application servers, and we do not collect, monitor, store, or transmit your personal data, credentials, or character sheets to any third party or external server.

2. Google Drive API Usage

The App integrates with Google Drive to let you back up and restore your character sheet data directly to your own Google account. This integration operates under the following conditions:

  • Scope of Access: The App requests the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file scope. This is a restricted, safe permission that only grants the App access to files that this App created (the Google Documents created by you when using StoneSys). The App cannot view, modify, or access any other files in your Google Drive.
  • Data Control: Your character data is saved directly from your browser to your own Google Drive. We do not store, intercept, or have access to this data.
  • Access Tokens: OAuth authentication tokens are held purely in your browser's temporary memory (RAM) during your active session and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Browser Local Storage

To provide a seamless experience, your character configuration and sheet status are saved locally in your own browser's localStorage. This data remains on your physical device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser cache or clicking "Reset character" in the App toolbar.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository where this project is hosted.

### The Psionicist

*You walked the inner path and woke the Way — the quiet power of a disciplined mind. No god granted it and no king can strip it from you. But it is yours alone to pay for, and the strain is real.*

**Duty:** The Way answers the still mind. Reach in fear or fury and it answers that, too, and the answer is worse.

**Look** — choose one per line:

- *Bearing:* utterly still, distant, serene, coiled inward
- *Dress:* a wanderer's plain wrap, a disciple's shaved simplicity, whatever draws no eye, small ritual tokens
- *Eyes:* fixed and calm, far away, unsettlingly steady, closed as often as open
- *Tell:* you answer questions not yet asked, a slight nosebleed after effort, you never quite startle, you touch your brow when you focus

**Gear** — choose as marked:

- *Focus:* a smooth meditation stone · a string of counting-beads · nothing at all; the mind is enough
- *Ward:* a plain staff (d6) · a hidden knife (d6) · a disciple's robes that hide much
- *Keepsake:* your master's final koan · a scar from the day the Way woke · a list of others who walk the path

#### Moves

You begin with the signature move, every fixed move, and 2 choice moves.

#### The Way  ·  *Signature*

> When you turn the mind's power on the world — to move, sense, shield, strike, or link.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The discipline works as you will it.
- **Failure:** The power slips your grip, and the strain is all you get for reaching.
- **▲ spend:** Hold 1 Focus (spend: extend the effect to another target / no one perceives that you did it / skim a surface thought or intent / brace against the strain of your next effort). / You sense another mind or power stirring nearby.
- **▼ costs:** The strain shows: take a setback die until you can still your mind. / A psion-sensitive, or something on the Gray, feels you reach.
- **◆ Triumph:** A surge: the discipline does far more than it should.
- **✶ Despair:** You overreach and burn out. Lose this discipline until you rest deeply, or worse.

**Hold — Focus.** Spend 1 to: Extend the effect to a second target or a wider area. / Work the power so subtly that no one perceives it. / Skim a surface thought or read intent. / Brace your mind against the strain of your next effort.

#### The Unseen Art  ·  *Fixed*

> Because the Way needs no word, gesture, or component:

You can use your power without anyone knowing its source — but other psionicists, and certain beasts and horrors, can feel the Way whenever you draw on it. To them, you are never truly hidden.

#### Mindlink  ·  *Choice*

> When you open a silent link to a willing ally.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **easy**.

- **Success:** You speak mind to mind, wordless and unheard, for as long as you both concentrate.
- **Failure:** The link will not hold, or opens to the wrong mind.
- **▲ spend:** Link a whole group at once. / Share not just words but a sense or an image.
- **▼ costs:** Something else brushes the link. / The effort leaves you both a little raw.
- **◆ Triumph:** The link is effortless and deep; you act as one.
- **✶ Despair:** You link to a mind that was waiting for you to.

#### Read  ·  *Choice*

> When you turn your senses on another mind to learn what drives it.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** Ask the GM one, truthfully: what does this person most want? most fear? what are they hiding right now?
- **Failure:** Their mind is guarded, or you read a lie they believe themselves.
- **▲ spend:** Ask another. / They never feel you looking.
- **▼ costs:** They feel the intrusion. / What you see clings to you.
- **◆ Triumph:** You see them whole, guards and all.
- **✶ Despair:** There is something in there that looks back.

#### Kinesis  ·  *Choice*

> When you move, hold, or break a thing with your mind alone.

**Roll +INT** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** It moves as you will, within reason.
- **Failure:** It resists you, or moves wrong.
- **▲ spend:** Do it with force enough to matter in a fight. / Do it to several small things at once.
- **▼ costs:** The strain bites: setback until you rest. / You move it, and knock something else loose.
- **◆ Triumph:** You move something no arm could.
- **✶ Despair:** The power lashes back at you or an ally.

#### Mind Blade  ·  *Choice*

> When you strike at a mind directly, or raise a shield of pure will against one.

**Roll +CON** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** Your strike lands past any armor, or your shield turns the attack.
- **Failure:** It fails, and the backlash is yours to bear.
- **▲ spend:** Stagger the foe: they act badly next. / Shield an ally as well as yourself.
- **▼ costs:** The clash of wills costs you: setback until you rest.
- **◆ Triumph:** You lay a mind low without touching the body.
- **✶ Despair:** Your own mind takes the wound instead.

- The Legion's leaders would make you a weapon of interrogation; ____ argues that you are more than that.

#### Advancement

**Basic** (5 XP each):

- ☐ Take another Psionicist move.
- ☐ Take another Psionicist move.
- ☐ Take a move from another playbook.
- ☐ Take a move from another playbook.
- ☐ Increase your maximum held Focus by 1.
- ☐ Open a new discipline: name a new use of the Way you can attempt.
- ☐ Train a discipline you lean on: it upgrades 2 dice to proficiency (never more than its stat).
- ☐ Deepen your calm: once per session, ignore the strain of one working.

**Advanced:**

- ☐ Raise one stat by 1 (max 5). Rare, and only at a true turning point.
- ☐ Master a discipline so wholly it no longer strains you at all.
- ☐ Take students: found a Bastion (a quiet school of the Way) within or beside the Legion.

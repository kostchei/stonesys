### The Sewer Scavenger

*You know the city from underneath — the drains, the crypts, the smugglers' tunnels, the Underway beneath the Black Cat itself. What the city throws away, you find; where the city cannot follow, you go. You have a stronger stomach and a longer memory for dark places than anyone alive should.*

**Duty:** The under-city keeps you fed and keeps the band's secrets. But there are older things down there than tunnels, and they are starting to notice you.

**Look** — choose one per line:

- *Bearing:* hunched and wary, unhurried, always sniffing the air, eerily at home in the dark
- *Dress:* oilskins that have seen everything, layers against the damp and stink, practical and pocketed, rags that hide good tools
- *Marks:* scars from things that bit back, a permanent grime you've made peace with, a rash from the deep water, a lucky trinket dredged from the muck
- *Eyes:* dark-adjusted, quick and darting, flat and patient, seeing more than they should

**Gear** — choose as marked:

- *Tool:* a long gaff-hook (d6) · a shuttered lantern and flint · a pry-bar and rope
- *Kit:* a bag of sorted finds · vials of the deep water's cures and poisons · chalk, string, and a nose for direction
- *Guard:* a heavy knife (d6) · a thick oilskin that turns claws · a whistle only certain rats obey

#### Moves

You begin with the signature move, every fixed move, and 2 choice moves.

#### Down Below  ·  *Signature*

> When you go into the drains, tunnels, crypts, and forgotten ways under the city.

**Roll +CON** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** You find the way through, or the thing you came for, and you endure the dark to do it.
- **Failure:** The under-city turns you around, or takes something from you for the passage.
- **▲ spend:** Hold 1 Findings (spend: a hidden route to where you need to be / a valuable thing the muck was keeping / you sense the hazard before it reaches you / a bolt-hole to vanish into). / You learn what else has been using these ways lately.
- **▼ costs:** You bring something up with you, or leave a trail down for it to follow. / The deep water sickens you: a setback die until you can dry out and rest.
- **◆ Triumph:** You find a way or a treasure no one else knows exists.
- **✶ Despair:** You have woken, or been noticed by, something old and hungry below.

**Hold — Findings.** Spend 1 to: A hidden route brings you out exactly where you need to be. / You dredge up something valuable the muck was hiding. / You sense a hazard, a pit, or a lurker before it reaches you. / You know a bolt-hole to disappear into right now.

#### Keeper of the Underway  ·  *Fixed*

> Because the map of the tunnels beneath the Black Cat lives in your head alone:

You can move the band unseen under half the quarter, and hide what must not be found. Serve the den well this way and raise your Standing; lead something up the tunnels, or let the Underway be discovered, and lower it. What you gatekeep, you are also responsible for.

#### Scrounge  ·  *Choice*

> When you pick over a place — a wreck, a ruin, a rich man's midden — for what's worth taking.

**Roll +INT** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** You come away with something useful, valuable, or telling.
- **Failure:** There's nothing good here, and looking has cost you time or safety.
- **▲ spend:** You find exactly the odd thing that solves a current problem. / You find it without anyone noticing you were here.
- **▼ costs:** What you found is claimed, cursed, or watched. / You disturbed something that will come looking.
- **◆ Triumph:** You unearth a real prize, worth a season's rent.
- **✶ Despair:** The treasure is bait, and you've sprung the trap.

#### Cast-Iron Gut  ·  *Choice*

> When filth, poison, disease, foul air, or sheer exhaustion would drop a softer body.

**Roll +CON** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** You shrug it off and keep going.
- **Failure:** Even you have a limit, and you've just found it.
- **▲ spend:** Carry someone else through it too. / You know a cure or a trick to share.
- **▼ costs:** You endure it now, but it will catch up with you later.
- **◆ Triumph:** You outlast a thing that should have killed you.
- **✶ Despair:** It gets into you, and it does not let go easily.

#### Never Lost  ·  *Choice*

> When you need to find the way in the dark, the fog, or the maze of the under-city.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **easy**.

- **Success:** You know where you are and how to get where you're going.
- **Failure:** The way is stranger than you remember; something has changed down here.
- **▲ spend:** Find the fastest route, or the safest, your pick. / Know where a pursuer will lose your trail.
- **▼ costs:** The route is open, but not empty.
- **◆ Triumph:** You could walk it blind, and nearly have to.
- **✶ Despair:** The map in your head is wrong now, and you don't know why.

#### Rat-Friend  ·  *Choice*

> When you deal with the vermin, strays, and half-wild things that rule the low places:

Take a boost die to pass among them, read their alarm, or turn them from you. The rats of this city are cleverer and better-organized than anyone admits, and some of them are listening.

**Bonds** — fill in at least two:

- ____ cannot stand the stink of me, and tries to hide it, and fails.
- I showed ____ one secret way, and I watch to see if they keep it secret.
- ____ nursed me through a deep-water fever, and I have not forgotten.
- I found something down below that belongs to ____, and I haven't decided whether to give it back.

**Community tie** — choose one:

- The Black Cat is safe because you keep the Underway; ____ is the only one who fully grasps what that costs you.
- When the band must vanish, ____ trusts you to take them down and bring them up alive.
- Something below has begun to take an interest in the den; ____ helps you keep watch on the dark.

**Tracks:**

- **Standing** (0–3, start 1). How much the band relies on you and your hidden ways.

#### Advancement

**Basic** (5 XP each):

- ☐ Take another Scavenger move.
- ☐ Take another Scavenger move.

- ☐ Take a move from another playbook.

- ☐ Map a new deep: name a stretch of the under-city you now know cold.
- ☐ Train a move you lean on: it upgrades 2 dice to proficiency (never more than its stat).
- ☐ Raise your Standing by 1 by getting the whole band out of a trap through the tunnels.

- ☐ Raise one stat by 1 (max 5). Rare, and only at a true turning point.
- ☐ Go deep for good: follow the Underway to whatever waits at its end, and come back changed.
- ☐ Claim the under-quarter: found a Bastion (a hidden warren) beneath the Black Cat that the band controls.

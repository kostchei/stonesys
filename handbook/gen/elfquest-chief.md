### Chieftain

*The tribe is yours to lead, which means it is yours to keep alive. Every mouth, every cub, every quarrel and hunt and hard winter comes home to you. You did not ask to carry them all, or perhaps you fought for the right. Either way the lodge is yours now, and the weight of it never sets down.*

**Duty:** Lead, and they live; falter, and they scatter or starve. The tribe's fate is the measure of you, and it is never off your shoulders.

- *Eyes:* reading every face, hard when they must be, tired but kind, always counting the tribe

- *Office:* the lodge and its hearth · the tribe's stores in your keeping · a token every elf here respects
- *Burden:* a hard decision you've not yet made · a rival who wants the lodge · the names of everyone the winters have taken

#### Moves

You begin with the signature move, every fixed move, and 2 choice moves.

#### Hold Them Together  ·  *Signature*

> When you rally, command, or steady the tribe in a hard moment.

**Roll +CHA** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** They follow your lead, and hold.
- **Failure:** They waver, split, or defy you, and the GM shows you the cost.
- **▲ spend:** Hold 1 Resolve (spend: hearten them for a boost die on what comes / cut to the truth of a quarrel / rouse them to act as one body / quell a panic before it spreads). / You feel exactly where the tribe's fear or anger truly sits.
- **▼ costs:** Someone resents the order, and will remember. / Holding them costs you: a favor spent, a doubt sown, your own strength drained.
- **◆ Triumph:** They would follow you into the long-cold itself.
- **✶ Despair:** The tribe fractures, or turns against you, at the worst possible time.

**Hold — Resolve.** Spend 1 to: Hearten the tribe: they take a boost die on what comes next. / Cut through a quarrel to the truth beneath it. / Rouse them to move and act as one body. / Quell a rising panic before it spreads.

#### The Tribe Looks to You  ·  *Fixed*

> Because every life in the Holt is yours to answer for:

When you spend yourself for the tribe — take the hard watch, the hard choice, the blame — they trust you the more for it, and rally to you when it counts. But you carry them always; their losses are your wounds, and you cannot set the weight down while you hold the lodge.

#### Judge  ·  *Choice*

> When you weigh a dispute, a claim, or a person's word before the tribe.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** Ask the GM one: who here speaks true? what does each truly want? what will heal this, and what will worsen it?
- **Failure:** You misjudge it, and your ruling makes the wound deeper.
- **▲ spend:** Ask another. / Your judgment satisfies even the side that lost.
- **▼ costs:** Whatever you decide, someone will carry a grudge for it.
- **◆ Triumph:** You render a judgment the whole tribe knows to be just.
- **✶ Despair:** You rule badly on something that matters, and it festers.

#### War-Leader  ·  *Choice*

> When you lead the tribe in a fight or a defense of the Holt.

**Roll +STR** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The tribe fights well under your hand; the line holds or the charge lands.
- **Failure:** The plan breaks, or holding costs the tribe dearly.
- **▲ spend:** Hold 1 Resolve. / Place your fighters where they will do the most and lose the least.
- **▼ costs:** A gain here leaves a gap there. / The fight goes longer and harder than it should.
- **◆ Triumph:** You win the day with the tribe whole.
- **✶ Despair:** The fight turns to disaster, and elves you knew do not come home.

#### Speak for the Tribe  ·  *Choice*

> When you parley with outsiders — another tribe, a human, a troll — as the tribe's voice.

**Roll +CHA** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** You reach terms the tribe can live with.
- **Failure:** The talk fails, or the terms cost more than you can pay.
- **▲ spend:** You gain a small edge — a favor, a truce, a warning of their intent. / They come away respecting the Holt.
- **▼ costs:** The terms bind you tighter than you'd like. / One side leaves looking for a reason to break faith.
- **◆ Triumph:** You forge an alliance or a peace that changes the Holt's fortunes.
- **✶ Despair:** The parley turns to insult or bloodshed, and you carry it home.

#### Advancement

**Basic** (5 XP each):

- ☐ Take another Chief move.
- ☐ Take another Chief move.
- ☐ Take a move from another playbook.
- ☐ Take a move from another playbook.
- ☐ Increase your maximum held Resolve by 1.
- ☐ Name a second: choose an elf who can lead in your stead, and lean on them.
- ☐ Train a move you lean on: it upgrades 2 dice to proficiency (never more than its stat).
- ☐ Bind an old wound: heal a rift in the tribe that has festered for years.

**Advanced:**

- ☐ Raise one stat by 1 (max 5). Rare, and only at a true turning point.
- ☐ Lay down the lodge: pass leadership on your own terms, resolving the fallout in play, and become an elder.
- ☐ Unite the tribes: forge a greater people from many holts, founding a Bastion (a gathering-place) that outlasts you.

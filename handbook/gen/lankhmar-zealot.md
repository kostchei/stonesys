### The Zealot of the Street of Gods

*On the street where a hundred gods keep their shrines and beg for worshippers, you found one that answered — a small, hungry, spiteful god that most walk past. It speaks to you. It keeps its bargains. It is also insane, in the way only a god can be, and you are its voice in the city.*

**Duty:** Your god is real, near, and listening. Its favor is a blade with no handle; carry it wrong and it cuts you.

- *Burden:* a taboo you must never break · a prophecy you half-understand · a debt of Favor already owed

#### Moves

You begin with the signature move, every fixed move, and 2 choice moves.

#### It Answers  ·  *Signature*

> When you call on your small god under pressure and offer it something it wants.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The god answers — a sign, a small miracle, a whispered truth true to its strange nature.
- **Failure:** Silence, or an answer that curdles; the GM makes a move.
- **▲ spend:** Hold 1 Favor (spend per Part III: bless a roll that serves your god, ask an omen, turn a weak holy result stronger, ward off a small curse or fear, or declare a shrine or sign of your god is near). / The god throws in a spiteful truth about someone present.
- **▼ costs:** The god demands its due now — a taboo upheld, an offering paid, a cruelty done. / A rival god or its faithful takes notice of yours.
- **◆ Triumph:** Your god is delighted; its answer is generous, and a little terrifying.
- **✶ Despair:** You have offended it. Mark Wrath, and it does not forget.

**Hold — Favor.** Spend 1 to: Add a boost die to a roll that clearly serves your god's nature. / Ask the GM for an omen or sign. / Turn a bare success on a holy act into a strong one. / Ward an ally from a small curse, fear, or ill fortune. / Declare a shrine, token, or sign of your god is near.

#### Its Voice in the City  ·  *Fixed*

> Because you carry a real and jealous god (chapter 13: Pray for Aid, Devotion, Wrath):

You may perform its rites and seek its aid, gaining Devotion by upholding its way at cost. But its taboos bind you utterly and its temper is a god's temper; break faith and mark Wrath. Other gods of the street, and their zealots, will know your god by the reek of it on you.

#### Sermon  ·  *Choice*

> When you preach, exhort, or terrify a crowd in your god's name.

**Roll +CHA** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** They listen, and move the way you push them.
- **Failure:** They turn on you, or laugh, which is worse.
- **▲ spend:** Win a follower or two to your god. / Plant a fear or a fervor that lingers after you go.
- **▼ costs:** You draw the eye of the watch or a rival cult. / Your god likes the attention a little too much.
- **◆ Triumph:** The crowd is yours, and your god's, body and soul, for now.
- **✶ Despair:** You've made a martyr, an enemy, or a scene that follows you.

#### Read the Omens  ·  *Choice*

> When you read the signs your god scatters through the world — entrails, weather, the fall of the fog.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** You glean a true thing about what is coming or what is hidden.
- **Failure:** The signs are murk, or a lie your god finds funny.
- **▲ spend:** A second reading, clearer. / You learn how to turn the omen aside.
- **▼ costs:** Reading it drew the notice of what you read. / The truth is one you did not want.
- **◆ Triumph:** You see the shape of the thing whole.
- **✶ Despair:** The omen is of your own doom, and your god seems pleased.

#### Wrath of the Small God  ·  *Choice*

> When you call your god's spite down on someone who has earned it:

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** Ill luck, sickness, or a small curse settles on your target as your god delights.
- **Failure:** Nothing — or the curse rebounds on you or yours.
- **▲ spend:** The curse is visible enough to frighten witnesses into belief.
- **▼ costs:** Your god demands a price for its cruelty: an offering, or mark 1 Wrath. / The target learns who cursed them.
- **◆ Triumph:** The curse bites deep and undeniably.
- **✶ Despair:** Your god enjoys this so much it wants more, from you, now.

#### Sanctuary  ·  *Choice*

> When you claim a threshold, a shrine, or a circle in your god's name:

Declare the space warded for a scene. Those your god would protect take a boost die within it against harm you name; those it hates find it hostile ground. Holding it may cost an offering.

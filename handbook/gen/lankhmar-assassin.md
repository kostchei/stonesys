### The Brotherhood Assassin

*You kill for the Brotherhood, patiently and well. It is a craft, not a rage: the study, the wait, the single clean moment. The Brotherhood trained you, owns your contracts, and would kill you slowly if you ever kept a coin or a secret it was owed. You are trying, quietly, to want something more than the next name on the list.*

**Duty:** The contract is sacred and the Brotherhood is watching. Take a life it did not sanction, or spare one it did, and you become a contract yourself.

**Look** — choose one per line:

- *Bearing:* perfectly forgettable, still and patient, coldly gentle, always slightly elsewhere
- *Dress:* whatever the crowd wears, a servant's or tradesman's guise, dark and close for the work, unremarkable good quality
- *Eyes:* calm, measuring, tired of it, kind, which is the frightening part
- *Mark:* a Brotherhood cipher, hidden, a tally you keep in secret, a scar from the one who nearly got you first, nothing; that is the point

**Gear** — choose as marked:

- *Instrument:* a thin blade made for one thrust (d6) · a garrote and gloves · a set of poisons and a needle
- *Cover:* a tradesman's tools and role · a servant's livery · the clothes and papers of a traveler
- *Secret:* a contract you have not fulfilled · the name of your own Brotherhood handler · a life you were paid to take and didn't

#### Moves

You begin with the signature move, every fixed move, and 2 choice moves.

#### The Clean Kill  ·  *Signature*

> When you strike at a mark you have studied, in the moment you have waited for.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The mark falls, and no alarm is raised in the same breath.
- **Failure:** The moment is wrong — the blow fails, or it lands but everything else goes loud.
- **▲ spend:** Hold 1 Patience (spend: no one saw who did it / you're already gone before they find the body / it looks like accident or another's hand / you have your escape route walked and clear). / You learn a second useful truth about the mark's world.
- **▼ costs:** A witness, a trace, or a survivor who can name you. / The kill was watched, by the watch, a rival, or the Brotherhood itself.
- **◆ Triumph:** The perfect contract: no witness, no trace, no suspicion that it was murder at all.
- **✶ Despair:** You've killed the wrong person, or been made, and the fallout is already moving.

**Hold — Patience.** Spend 1 to: No one saw who struck the blow. / You are already gone before the body is found. / It reads as accident, illness, or another hand entirely. / Your escape route is walked, clear, and waiting.

#### The Brotherhood's Reach  ·  *Fixed*

> When you draw on the Brotherhood for a poison, a safe house, a forged writ, or a name (Invoke Patron — chapter 12):

The Brotherhood provides, and adds 1 Debt. It keeps a ledger of every contract, coin, and secret. Cheat it, refuse a sanctioned kill, or kill outside its sanction, and the ledger balances itself with your life.

#### Stalk  ·  *Choice*

> When you shadow a mark to learn their life and their weak hour.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** Ask the GM one: when are they alone? whom do they trust? what is their fear? Learn the moment to strike.
- **Failure:** You learn little, and risk being noticed for the watching.
- **▲ spend:** Ask another. / You could walk into their house as someone they'd welcome.
- **▼ costs:** They sense they are being watched. / Getting this close cost you a trace left behind.
- **◆ Triumph:** You know their life better than they do.
- **✶ Despair:** They were the hunter, and you the mark, all along.

#### Unseen  ·  *Choice*

> When you move, wait, or leave unnoticed, becoming part of the scenery.

**Roll +DEX** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** No one marks you coming, staying, or going.
- **Failure:** An eye catches you where you should not be.
- **▲ spend:** Move another with you, just as unseen. / Leave nothing behind that says you were there.
- **▼ costs:** You pass, but leave one thread that could be pulled. / You're in, with no easy way back out.
- **◆ Triumph:** You were never there at all, as far as anyone will ever prove.
- **✶ Despair:** The one who sees you is the one who most matters.

#### Venoms  ·  *Choice*

> When you prepare, place, or identify a poison.

**Roll +INT** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** You have the right poison, and the means to place it unremarked.
- **Failure:** You lack it, or misjudge the dose, the timing, or the victim.
- **▲ spend:** Slow, deniable, easily mistaken for illness. / You carry its antidote, too.
- **▼ costs:** It leaves a trace a knowing physician could find. / You handle it carelessly and take a touch yourself.
- **◆ Triumph:** Undetectable and perfectly timed.
- **✶ Despair:** It takes the wrong person, or is traced straight to your hand.

#### Cold Mercy  ·  *Choice*

> When you choose, against the contract, to spare a life or stay your hand:

You may refuse or delay a kill. Take a boost die to make the sparing look like failure, accident, or another's mercy — but the Brotherhood keeps its ledger, and a spared mark is a debt that will come due, in Debt and in blood.

#### Embedment

*patron*

Start: Bond 1, Debt 2, Heat 0.

**Bonds** — fill in at least two:

- ____ knows what I do and has not turned me in; I do not fully understand why.
- The Brotherhood once handed me ____'s name, and I burned the contract instead.
- ____ believes there is a better person under the killer, and I let them believe it.
- I owe ____ for a death they helped me hide.
- If the Brotherhood ever marks ____, I will break with it, and we both half-know that.

**Community tie** — choose one:

- The Black Cat's enemies fear it because of what you can do quietly; ____ is uneasy about needing you.
- The Brotherhood would pay well for a blade already inside the band; ____ is the one you'll never accept a contract on.
- You have kept the band alive by removing threats they never learned about; ____ suspects, and says nothing.

**Tracks:**

- **Bond** (0–3, start 1). Standing with the Assassins' Brotherhood.
- **Debt** (0–6, start 2). At 6 the Brotherhood makes a major demand — a contract — then resets to 3.
- **Heat** (0–4, start 0). Suspicion from the watch, rivals, and the marked.

#### Advancement

**Basic** (5 XP each):

- ☐ Take another Assassin move.
- ☐ Take another Assassin move.
- ☐ Take a move from another playbook.
- ☐ Take a move from another playbook.
- ☐ Increase your maximum held Patience by 1.
- ☐ Perfect a signature method that leaves no evidence and no doubt.
- ☐ Train a move you lean on: it upgrades 2 dice to proficiency (never more than its stat).
- ☐ Balance your ledger: buy down your Brotherhood Debt by 1, permanently.

**Advanced:**

- ☐ Raise one stat by 1 (max 5). Rare, and only at a true turning point.
- ☐ Break the contract for good: leave the Brotherhood, resolving the fallout in play, and spend the rest of your life a marked one.
- ☐ Rise within it: take apprentices and a Bastion (a Brotherhood cell) of your own.

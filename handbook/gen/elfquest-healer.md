### Healer

*Yours is the magic of the living body — to knit the torn, cool the fever, coax a failing heart to beat on. In a small tribe pressed by a hard world, that gift is the thin line between the Holt and the long silence. They cannot lose you, and every one of them knows it, and so do you.*

**Duty:** Your hands are the tribe's shield against the wounds of the hunt and the wasting of the cold. What you can heal, you must; what you cannot, you carry.

#### Moves

You begin with the signature move, every fixed move, and 2 choice moves.

#### Healing Hands  ·  *Signature*

> When you lay hands on a hurt and use the magic of the living body to mend it.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The wound closes, the fever breaks, the failing body steadies.
- **Failure:** The hurt is beyond your reach here, or the mending demands a price you must choose to pay or refuse.
- **▲ spend:** Hold 1 Vigor (spend: draw out a poison or sickness as well / mend it without cost to yourself / rouse them to their feet, now / leave a lasting strength behind). / You learn exactly what harmed them, and what still threatens them.
- **▼ costs:** You take some of their hurt into yourself: a setback die until you rest. / The healing drains you deeply, or the hurt was hiding something worse.
- **◆ Triumph:** You pull them back from the very edge, whole.
- **✶ Despair:** The healing turns wrong, or they slip away despite you, and it marks you.

**Hold — Vigor.** Spend 1 to: Draw out a poison, sickness, or rot along with the wound. / Mend the hurt without taking any cost into yourself. / Rouse them to their feet and back into the moment, now. / Leave a lasting strength or resilience behind.

#### The Tribe's Life  ·  *Fixed*

> Because your gift is the Holt's shield against death:

The tribe protects you as they protect no one else; in a fight, elves will step between you and harm, and they will spend much to keep you safe. But you are precious and few — the tribe cannot afford to spend you rashly, and neither can you.

#### Cleanse  ·  *Choice*

> When you drive out a poison, a disease, or a wasting sickness.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The ill is drawn out; the body begins to right itself.
- **Failure:** It resists you, or the cure costs nearly as much as the sickness.
- **▲ spend:** Cleanse several who share the sickness. / Learn the source before it spreads further.
- **▼ costs:** Some of it clings to you: a setback until you rest. / The cure buys time, not a cure.
- **◆ Triumph:** You root the illness out entirely.
- **✶ Despair:** You drive it out of one and into yourself, or it was never the true sickness at all.

#### Steady the Mind  ·  *Choice*

> When you ease a grief, a terror, or a madness in someone's heart.

**Roll +CHA** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The storm in them quiets enough to think and act.
- **Failure:** You cannot reach them, or reaching them, you take on some of their pain.
- **▲ spend:** The calm lasts, and they trust you with what's beneath it. / You learn the root of their fear or grief.
- **▼ costs:** Their pain lingers in you afterward.
- **◆ Triumph:** You help them truly heal, not just endure.
- **✶ Despair:** You stir something in them, or in yourself, better left still.

#### Herbcraft  ·  *Choice*

> When you seek, prepare, or apply the plants and remedies of the wood.

**Roll +INT** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** You have or find the right herb, and know its use.
- **Failure:** It's out of season, out of reach, or you mistake one plant for another.
- **▲ spend:** Enough to spare, or to store against later need. / You know a second, cleverer use for it.
- **▼ costs:** Gathering it took you somewhere dangerous. / The remedy has a hard side effect.
- **◆ Triumph:** You find or brew a remedy of rare power.
- **✶ Despair:** You misjudge a plant, and the cure becomes the harm.

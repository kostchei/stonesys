### Stargazer

*You hold the tribe's memory and read the world's patterns — the wheel of the stars, the old lore, the signs that others walk past. You know the histories no one else recalls and glimpse, sometimes, the shape of what is coming. In a tribe fighting to survive the present, you are the one still watching the long dark and the far light.*

**Duty:** You carry what the tribe would otherwise forget, and see the storms before they break. Knowledge is a weight, and foresight a lonely one.

#### Moves

You begin with the signature move, every fixed move, and 2 choice moves.

#### Read the Sky  ·  *Signature*

> When you seek meaning in the stars, the old lore, or the deep patterns of the world.

**Roll +INT** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** You glean a true thing about what is coming, or what lies hidden.
- **Failure:** The signs are murk or contradiction, and reading them wrong will cost you.
- **▲ spend:** Hold 1 Insight (spend: a clearer omen of what approaches / true knowledge of a place, creature, or old history / a warning of a danger before it comes / the one right question the tribe should be asking). / You recall a piece of lore that bears directly on the moment.
- **▼ costs:** What you learn troubles you deeply, and the weight of it shows. / You fall to brooding on the signs while real danger moves in the now.
- **◆ Triumph:** A vision of startling clarity: you see the shape of the thing whole.
- **✶ Despair:** A true and terrible foreseeing, of a doom you may not be able to turn.

**Hold — Insight.** Spend 1 to: A clearer omen of what is coming. / True knowledge of a place, a creature, or a piece of old history. / A warning of a specific danger before it arrives. / The one right question the tribe most needs to ask.

#### Keeper of the Lore  ·  *Fixed*

> Because you hold the tribe's memory and the old knowledge:

When something ancient stirs, or a forgotten thing matters, the tribe turns to you, and you likely know of it — take a boost die to recall true lore about the old world, its powers, and its creatures. But you carry what others have let fall, and the knowing can be a lonely and a heavy thing.

#### Ancient Lore  ·  *Choice*

> When you search your memory and the old teachings about a creature, a place, a power, or the vanished past.

**Roll +INT** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** Ask the GM one: what is it, and where did it come from? what does it want or fear? how was it dealt with before?
- **Failure:** The lore is fragmentary or wrong, and acting on it will mislead you.
- **▲ spend:** Ask another. / You recall the old way to bind, wake, or ward it.
- **▼ costs:** The knowledge is dangerous to hold, or to have shown you hold it.
- **◆ Triumph:** You recall the whole tale, its truths and its warnings alike.
- **✶ Despair:** You misremember something old and powerful, badly.

#### Foresee  ·  *Choice*

> When you cast the stones, read the omens, and seek what is to come.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** You glimpse a true shape of what approaches, though never the whole of it.
- **Failure:** The future clouds, or shows you a lie that seems true.
- **▲ spend:** See how the thing might be turned aside. / See who or what is the hinge of it.
- **▼ costs:** The vision costs you: it clings, or it frightens those you tell. / Knowing draws the future's attention to you.
- **◆ Triumph:** A clear and useful foreseeing, with time to act on it.
- **✶ Despair:** You foresee a doom, and cannot tell if the seeing helps make it so.

#### Navigate by Star and Season  ·  *Choice*

> When you find the way, or the time, by the stars and the turning of the year.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** You know where you are, which way to go, and how the season stands.
- **Failure:** The sky is hidden or strange, and you are less sure than the tribe needs you to be.
- **▲ spend:** Find the swiftest way, or the safest, your pick. / Know how long you have before the season turns.
- **▼ costs:** The route is right, but longer or harder than hoped.
- **◆ Triumph:** You could guide the tribe across the whole world by the wheel of the stars.
- **✶ Despair:** You misread the sky, and lead the tribe astray.

- ☐ Raise a hall of memory: found a Bastion (a place of lore and star-watching) that keeps the old knowledge for many tribes.

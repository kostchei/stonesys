### Tree-Shaper

*The living wood answers you. Under your hands a sapling becomes a shelter, a branch becomes a bow, a wall of thorn rises between the tribe and the dark. You did not learn this so much as remember it, the way the forest remembers you. The Mother Tree itself is half your doing, and half your teacher.*

**Duty:** The green is not a tool but a partner; it gives as you give, and it remembers every cut. Shape with the forest, never merely against it.

- *Bearing:* rooted and calm, restless as new growth, patient as an oak, attuned, half-elsewhere
- *Look:* leaf and vine woven into hair, bark-stained hands, clothes grown as much as sewn, a green cast to the skin in certain light
- *Sign:* seeds carried everywhere, a living staff still budding, plants that lean toward you, the smell of sap and green
- *Eyes:* deep forest-green, far-seeing into the wood, gentle, old as the trees

**Gear** — choose as marked:

- *Focus:* a living, budding staff (d6) · a pouch of seeds for every need · hands and the green alone
- *Craft:* shaped-wood tools of great beauty · a garden that follows the tribe · grafts and cuttings of rare plants
- *Bond:* a sapling grown from the Mother Tree · a grove you tend from afar · a debt the forest owes you, or you it

#### Moves

You begin with the signature move, every fixed move, and 2 choice moves.

#### Shape the Living Wood  ·  *Signature*

> When you coax wood, root, or growing thing to grow, bend, and take the shape you need.

**Roll +INT** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The green answers: it grows, bends, and shapes to your will.
- **Failure:** It resists, twists, or the shaping draws more from you than you have to give.
- **▲ spend:** Hold 1 Growth (spend: shape it faster or larger than natural / make the shaping lasting and strong / have it bear fruit, shelter, or cover / weave a living barrier or bridge). / The wood tells you something true about the forest around you.
- **▼ costs:** The shaping drains you: a setback die until you rest among green things. / The living thing takes a shape you did not intend, or resents the forcing.
- **◆ Triumph:** A wonder of living craft, as though the forest wanted it made.
- **✶ Despair:** The growth runs wild, or the wood withers and dies at your touch.

**Hold — Growth.** Spend 1 to: Shape it faster or larger than nature would allow. / Make the shaping lasting, strong, and seamless. / Have it bear fruit, shelter, cover, or a living tool. / Weave a living barrier, wall, or bridge.

#### The Green Speaks  ·  *Fixed*

> Because the living wood is never wholly strange to you:

You feel the forest around you — its health, its moods, the paths through it, the things that move in it — take a boost die when that closeness serves you. But the green remembers what you do to it: shape carelessly, take without giving, and the forest grows slow to answer you.

#### Grow Shelter  ·  *Choice*

> When you raise a living home, a hidden den, or a wall of wood and thorn.

**Roll +INT** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The living growth rises to shelter or shield as you need.
- **Failure:** It grows too slow, too weak, or in the wrong shape for the need.
- **▲ spend:** It is well hidden, blending into the wood. / It has a way in or out only you know.
- **▼ costs:** It takes time you may not have. / Raising it drew on you or on the forest heavily.
- **◆ Triumph:** A living stronghold the tribe could hold for years.
- **✶ Despair:** The shelter fails at the moment it is most needed.

#### Coax the Forest  ·  *Choice*

> When you ask the wood for its hidden gifts — food, a safe path, clean water, a place to hide.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The forest yields what the tribe needs.
- **Failure:** The wood is barren here, or its gift comes with a hidden hook.
- **▲ spend:** Enough for the whole tribe. / The forest closes the path behind you against pursuit.
- **▼ costs:** What the forest gives, something else was guarding. / Taking it cost the wood, and it remembers.
- **◆ Triumph:** The forest opens itself to you as to an old friend.
- **✶ Despair:** You take too much, and the wood turns cold to the tribe.

#### Living Weapons  ·  *Choice*

> When you shape the wood into arms for the tribe — a bow, a spear, a lash of thorn.

**Roll +INT** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The green yields a weapon, keen and true.
- **Failure:** The wood will not take the shape, or the arming costs the forest dearly.
- **▲ spend:** Arm several of the tribe at once. / The weapon has a living virtue — it mends, it grips, it grows barbs.
- **▼ costs:** The weapon is fine but the shaping left you spent. / A living weapon has a will of its own.
- **◆ Triumph:** A living weapon that becomes part of its wielder.
- **✶ Despair:** The shaped wood turns in the hand that holds it.

- ☐ Take another Tree-Shaper move.

- ☐ Train a move you lean on: it upgrades 2 dice to proficiency (never more than its stat).
- ☐ Restore the green: heal a stretch of forest that had gone sick or cold.

- ☐ Raise one stat by 1 (max 5). Rare, and only at a true turning point.
- ☐ Wake the Mother Tree: rouse the ancient oak to a fuller, stranger life, with all that follows.
- ☐ Grow a great holt: found a Bastion (a living stronghold) that shelters far more than one tribe.

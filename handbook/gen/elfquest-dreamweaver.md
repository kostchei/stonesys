### Dreamweaver

*The tribe's silent voices are always with you — the wordless sending that binds elf to elf — and you hear them more deeply than anyone. You walk in dreams, soothe troubled spirits, and weave many minds into one. You are the tribe's shared heart made flesh, which is a gift and a burden that never quiets.*

**Duty:** You hold the tribe's inner life — its dreams, griefs, and silent bonds. What passes between minds passes through you, and you cannot fully close the door.

#### Moves

You begin with the signature move, every fixed move, and 2 choice moves.

#### Weave the Dream  ·  *Signature*

> When you reach another mind or spirit through the silent sending, through dream, or through shared feeling.

**Roll +CHA** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** You touch their mind — to send, to soothe, or to share — clear and true.
- **Failure:** The reaching slips, or opens you to what you meant only to touch.
- **▲ spend:** Hold 1 Reverie (spend: reach across a great distance / share a true memory or feeling whole / soothe a troubled or grieving spirit / weave several minds together as one). / You sense a hidden truth in them — a fear, a love, a lie they tell themselves.
- **▼ costs:** You brush a wound in them, and it aches in you afterward. / The reaching leaves you open; another mind, or something older, touches you back.
- **◆ Triumph:** A communion so deep it heals or reveals something long buried.
- **✶ Despair:** You touch something in the dark of their mind — or your own — that will not let go.

**Hold — Reverie.** Spend 1 to: Reach a mind across a great distance. / Share a true memory or feeling whole, beyond words. / Soothe a troubled, grieving, or panicked spirit. / Weave several minds together to act and feel as one.

#### Open to the Sending  ·  *Fixed*

> Because the tribe's silent voices are always with you:

You hear the wordless sending of your tribe at all times — you always know, roughly, that your bonded kin live and how they fare, and you can call to them in silence. But you cannot fully close the door; strong feelings near you press in on you, and a mind in agony is very hard for you to ignore.

#### Send  ·  *Choice*

> When you speak mind to mind in silence, to a bonded elf or a willing mind.

**Roll +CHA** · default difficulty **easy**.

- **Success:** Your thought reaches them, wordless and unheard by any other.
- **Failure:** The sending will not carry, or reaches a mind you did not mean.
- **▲ spend:** Send to several at once. / Send more than words — an image, a warning, a feeling entire.
- **▼ costs:** Something else brushes the sending. / The effort leaves you briefly open and raw.
- **◆ Triumph:** Effortless, deep, and instant, across any distance you know.
- **✶ Despair:** You send into a mind that was waiting, quiet, for you to.

#### Soothe  ·  *Choice*

> When you calm a terror, a grief, a rage, or a nightmare in someone's heart.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The storm in them quiets; they can think and rest and act again.
- **Failure:** You cannot reach them, or soothing them, you take the storm into yourself.
- **▲ spend:** The calm lasts, and they trust you with what lay beneath it. / You learn the true root of their pain.
- **▼ costs:** Their pain lingers in you a while.
- **◆ Triumph:** You help them truly let the pain go, not merely bury it.
- **✶ Despair:** You still one storm and wake another, in them or in you.

#### Dreamwalk  ·  *Choice*

> When you enter, read, or walk within a sleeper's dream.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** You move within their dream and learn or ease what you came for.
- **Failure:** The dream turns on you, or you cannot find your way back cleanly.
- **▲ spend:** Bring a truth back out with you. / Leave a gift in the dream — courage, a memory, peace.
- **▼ costs:** Something in the dream follows you toward waking. / You linger too long, and waking is hard.
- **◆ Triumph:** You walk their dream as if it were your own, and reshape it gently.
- **✶ Despair:** The dream is not only theirs, and the other dreamer is not kind.

- ☐ Weave the whole tribe into one waking mind at need, with all the peril that carries.
- ☐ Raise a dreaming-place: found a Bastion (a place of shared dream and memory) that holds the tribe's soul across generations.

### The Elemental Cleric

*You serve one of the raw elements — fire, earth, air, water, silt, or magma — not a god who wears a face and hears prayers kindly, but the vast thing itself. It is patient, and it does not love you. It bargains. And in a dying world, its gifts are the difference between a people who live and a people who become dust.*

**Duty:** The element gives, and the element demands. Keep its rites and its taboos, or find out what it is like to be forsaken by the very ground or sky.

#### Moves

You begin with the signature move, every fixed move, and 2 choice moves.

#### Elemental Rite  ·  *Signature*

> When you call on your element under pressure and offer it something that matters.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** The element answers with a sign or aid true to its nature.
- **Failure:** Only silence, or an answer you did not want; the GM makes a move.
- **▲ spend:** Hold 1 Favor (spend later per Part III: bless a roll that serves the element, ask an omen, turn a weak result stronger, ward off a minor spiritual harm, or declare a shrine or sign is near). / The aid is greater or lasts longer than you asked.
- **▼ costs:** The element demands visible obedience or a further offering, now. / A rival power feels you draw on the deep force.
- **◆ Triumph:** A surge of elemental power, freely given.
- **✶ Despair:** You have offended the element. Mark Wrath.

**Hold — Favor.** Spend 1 to: Add a boost die to a roll that clearly serves the element's nature. / Ask the GM for an omen or sign. / Turn a bare success on a holy act into a strong one. / Ward an ally from a minor curse, fear, or spiritual harm. / Declare a shrine, spring, ember, or sign of your element is near.

#### Sworn to the Element  ·  *Fixed*

> Because you have given yourself to the element (chapter 13: Pray for Aid, Devotion, Wrath):

You can perform its rites and seek its aid, and you gain Devotion by upholding its way at cost. But its taboos bind you absolutely; break one and mark Wrath. The element is not a friend. It is a force you have learned to bargain with.

#### Shape the Element  ·  *Choice*

> When you command your element where it is present in the scene.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** It moves to your will: the fire leaps, the water gathers, the earth shifts, the wind rises.
- **Failure:** It will not heed you here, or it heeds too well.
- **▲ spend:** Shape it larger, or to two ends at once. / Do it without spending from your Favor.
- **▼ costs:** The element exacts a small toll for the command. / It does as you say and a little more.
- **◆ Triumph:** You command it as though you were part of it.
- **✶ Despair:** The element turns your command back on you.

#### Elemental Sense  ·  *Choice*

> When you reach out to feel your element in the land around you.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **easy**.

- **Success:** You sense it: where the water hides, where the fire will spread, where the ground is hollow or the wind will turn.
- **Failure:** The element is silent to you here, and the silence itself may mean something.
- **▲ spend:** Sense it far off, or in fine detail. / Feel another power that is near it.
- **▼ costs:** Reaching out, you brush something that reaches back.
- **◆ Triumph:** You feel the whole shape of your element across the region.
- **✶ Despair:** You sense your element being defiled, and it recoils through you.

#### Cleanse or Scour  ·  *Choice*

> When you turn your element to heal and purify, or to burn and scour away.

**Roll +WIS** · default difficulty **average**.

- **Success:** Wounds close, poison and rot are washed out, or corruption is burned clean — as fits your element.
- **Failure:** It is beyond your element's power here, or the cure costs as much as the ill.
- **▲ spend:** Tend several at once. / Leave a lasting blessing on the place or person.
- **▼ costs:** The rite draws on you: setback until you rest. / Spend 1 Favor or mark a small Wrath to finish it.
- **◆ Triumph:** A cleansing so complete it becomes a sign the Legion will speak of.
- **✶ Despair:** You scour away more than the sickness.

#### Advancement

**Basic** (5 XP each):

- ☐ Take another Elemental Cleric move.
- ☐ Take another Elemental Cleric move.
- ☐ Take a move from another playbook.
- ☐ Take a move from another playbook.
- ☐ Deepen a rite: name a greater working of your element you can attempt at Daunting difficulty.
- ☐ Raise your Devotion by 1 through a rite or sacrifice that costs you.
- ☐ Train a move you lean on: it upgrades 2 dice to proficiency (never more than its stat).
- ☐ Make peace with the element: reduce your Wrath by 1 through pilgrimage or restitution.

**Advanced:**

- ☐ Raise one stat by 1 (max 5). Rare, and only at a true turning point.
- ☐ Become a Chosen: the element marks you as its instrument, with all the danger that carries.
- ☐ Raise a shrine: found a Bastion (a holy place of your element) for the Legion to gather around.
